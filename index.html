Terms & Conditions

To qualify for a loan, a student must meet one of the two criteria:

  1. Provide collateral worth at least 40% of the requested amount (e.g. phone, laptop, TV, motorbike)
    • Repay in 30 days with 20% interest.
    • Late? An additional 20% is added.
  2. Be guaranteed by HESLB and show financial proof. Max: TZS 50,000.

Note: Non-repayment may result in sale of collateral.
